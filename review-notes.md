# Skill Core Labs v2 review notes

## Current direction

The site now follows Claude's harmonic field direction with a Scrib3-inspired type and color treatment: black base, dusty-pink accent, wide but thinner display typography, tight body copy, technical mono labels, and immersive membrane motion.

## Positioning correction

AI is now the product wedge. Markets, science, art, music, and culture are framed as proof that the claim record generalizes, not as equal headline markets.

## Homepage copy

- Title: `Skill Core Labs | Evidence Infrastructure for AI Systems`
- Core thesis: `Every AI output makes a claim. Reality decides what it was worth.`
- Universality section: `AI is the wedge. Claims are the substrate.`

## Visual discipline

- Fonts: Variable Archivo for a wide but crisper display voice, Inter Tight for body, IBM Plex Mono for technical labels. Display weights were reduced so the site feels sharper and less blocky while still nodding to Scrib3's custom OwnersWide/Pack/technical mix.
- Color: black, soft white, muted gray, and Scrib3-style dusty pink `#D7ABC5` as the true accent.
- No rainbow domain palette.
- Canvas is decorative, pointer-free, and reduced-motion safe.

## Page roles

- `index.html`: AI-first production homepage.
- `index-v2.html`: redirect to production homepage for legacy review links.
- `index-focused.html`: AI-assisted market decision wedge.
- `architecture.html`: operating spine and audit record.
- `products.html`: AI evidence infrastructure product family.
- `pilot.html`: clear pilot conversion page.
- `all-together.html`: review board.
